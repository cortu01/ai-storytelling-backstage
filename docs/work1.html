The Story

Once a famous vampire racer, Jean-Marc was left heartbroken after his daughter Lola was murdered by a mysterious group of spies . Lola, a skilled hacker, was a member of the Bat faction in the midst of a bitter war with the Bunny faction . As the conflict between the two factions escalated, a spy network of animal code names became more active in gathering intelligence and carrying out covert operations. Even after her death, Lola joined the Bat team as a ghost hacker to help track down her killers.

During the escalating conflict, two members of the Cat faction were murdered, and the Bat team suspected the Bunnies were responsible. In the chaos that ensued, Lola was wrongly accused of the murders and was forced to go on the run as a ghost to evade capture while investigating her own death.

As tensions rose, the spy network decided to host an annual baseball game , which served as a cover for a covert mission to uncover the truth behind the Cat faction murders. Unexpectedly, the Bunnies started to shift back into human form while playing the game, something they hadn't been able to do in years due to their lack of emotional connection to humanity.

Meanwhile, Jean-Marc , driven by his grief, sought revenge on Lola's killers and discovered that his arch-nemesis, Lola's stepfather Lord Roshan Roshan , was the head of the spy group responsible. But the vampire soon learned that the spy group was not the true enemy, as they were hired by a rabbit who wanted to rule the world by taking Lola's crown.

On the other hand, Lola's pet rabbit turned out to be the reason for her death . After becoming a wandering ghost, she joined a time-traveling society to become an assassin and revenge on Jean-Marc, but in the end, accidentally killed him and his friend at a bar with poisoned drinks, leading to her transformation back into a human .

Back to the present, a meteor fell near a rabbit village , and a group of spies suddenly appeared, forcing the rabbits to flee to a nearby mansion. Inside, they found a strange box, and a ghost-like female named Laura appeared , who asked for their help to stop the spies and offered the clue to an ancient and powerful crown. With Laura's guidance, the rabbits found the crown but accidentally broke the seal, releasing a vampire trapped inside .

In the end, the Bat and Bunny factions put aside their differences to stop the rogue Rat faction, who were planning a massive attack on both groups. With Lola's help and the cooperation of the spy network, the factions were able to infiltrate the Rat stronghold and prevent the attack. In the aftermath, the factions vowed to promote peace and understanding between their groups, and the spy network expanded their reach to other planets, spreading their rule to all corners of the universe .
